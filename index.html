Welcome to ToadSkool!

ToadSkool is a mushroom ID game that's as close to real mushroom hunting as possible without getting your shoes dirty.

Remember, some mushrooms are deadly so never eat a mushroom unless you are 100% sure of what it is and have identified it using multiple sources. If you eat something and get sick, its on you.

I sometimes describe mushrooms as "edible raw", while this is true you never know what else is on the mushroom so it's always a best practice to thoroughly wash and cook mushrooms before eating them.
